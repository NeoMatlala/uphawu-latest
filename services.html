Our Services

Energy Solutions

With the growing concern over reliable and cost effective energy sources, Uphawu Industrial Holdings has partnered with Cegasa (A battery manufacturer based in Spain) to offer Lithium ion and Zinc Air Alkaline batteries suitable for diverse applications.

  • Lithium ion batteries- Industrial cleaning equipment , Multi-functional lifting equipment , off grid installations
  • Zinc air alkaline batteries- security systems, electric fencing, railway and road traffic signalling, telecommunications , navigation aids

Gas Installations, Inspection & Maintenance

We cater for both large and small companies. We are skilled at performing vapour and liquid gas installations. Our technicians are experienced and can handle all installation issues efficiently . Our precautionary measures include checking out that there are zero possibilities of gas pocket formation. Pressure regulators, shut-off valves, safety valves, flame arrestors and specialty burners are all critical components that ensure a safe industrial gas installation.
