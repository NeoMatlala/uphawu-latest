About Us

Uphawu Industrial Holdings (UIH) functions as a materials supply and consultancy institution. We conduct destructive & non-destructive testing, provide metallurgical advice and consultancy to ensure improved production of quality products.

UIH also serves as a supplier of various industrial commodities across various sectors, these being safety wear as well as consumables. This wing of the company has shown significant growth since its inception and continues to grow from strength to strength.

With the prevailing uncertainty of electricity , a further need to look into storage of energy has been unavoidable . UIH is therefore focusing on providing customised energy alternatives to various industries in the form of energy storage solutions and the replacement of electricity demands with LPG gas.

Uphawu Metallurgical Services (PTY) LTD was established in 2013.

The company began as a sole mechanical testing service provider and later expanded into other avenues of material testing as well as industrial supplies.
